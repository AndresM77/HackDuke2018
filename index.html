Sourced

What is Sourced

A community unrestricted by barriers such as classes, schools, and districts.

A social media platform dedicated to cultivating a community of learning through peer-to-peer education.

A community where you can learn what you want now and with others, and not by yourself after a 40-hour course.

What does Sourced offer

Communities for learning based on topics/issues users are interested in.

A repository of crowd-sourced educational materials.

Crowd-funded projects based on your interests and passions.

Professional profiles developed from user activity.

Centralized location to find information and people based on your interests.
